Our Story

Hopeful Grounds began with a simple belief: that coffee has the power to bring people together and make a real difference. Founded in the heart of the Twin Cities, we set out to create a place where every cup served could fuel something greater. With locations in downtown St. Paul and Minneapolis, and a growing online community, we’ve built more than just a coffee shop—we’ve built a movement. As passionate roasters and proud community members, we craft premium blends that not only delight coffee lovers but also drive support for children's healthcare initiatives.
As we grew, so did our mission. We started hosting monthly events—like live music nights, community fundraisers, and family-friendly gatherings—turning our shops into vibrant spaces of hope, joy, and connection. Every bag of beans, every latte, and every donation fuels brighter futures for children and families. At Hopeful Grounds, this is more than coffee—it’s a cause, a community, and a celebration of giving back.

Our Mission

At Hopeful Grounds, our mission is to blend exceptional coffee with extraordinary compassion, rooted in our Christian values of love, service, and hope. Every cup we craft, every bean, and every gathering we create is an opportunity to serve others and reflect the care that Christ calls us to share. We are committed to supporting children’s healthcare and uplifting families walking through difficult medical journeys. Through every purchase and every act of generosity, we work to ease burdens, offer encouragement, and bring hope where it’s needed most.
Your support matters. 100% of donations go directly toward children's healthcare initiatives, helping us extend love, care, and vital resources to families in our community. Together, we can turn everyday moments—like enjoying a great cup of coffee—into life-changing impact.
